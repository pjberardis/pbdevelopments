Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

PB Developments respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

Information We Collect

Depending on the application, PB Developments may collect information necessary to provide app functionality, improve performance, and support users.

  • Information voluntarily entered by users
  • Device information required for app functionality
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information
  • Notification preferences, if enabled

Information We Do Not Collect

PB Developments does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Some applications may use trusted third-party services to provide functionality, improve reliability, or support app features.

  • Apple Push Notification Service
  • Firebase
  • Google Services

These services maintain their own privacy policies and data practices.

Basketball Scorekeeping

The Basketball Scorekeeping application is designed to assist teams, leagues, coaches, and scorekeepers with game management, scorekeeping, statistics, schedules, standings, and reports.

Information entered into the Basketball Scorekeeping application is used solely to provide scorekeeping and league management functionality. PB Developments does not sell or share this information for marketing purposes.

Children's Privacy

PB Developments applications are not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization where required.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to help protect information stored or processed by PB Developments applications.
